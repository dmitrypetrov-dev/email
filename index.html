There is a global competition to adapt and adopt
unmanned, and increasingly autonomous systems and
human machine learning.

In this session, we will address:

How a “Prototype Warfare” approach assists defense
acquisition.

Why increased human machines learning reduces risk and
secures future operational advantage.

How autonomy research outputs and COTS unmanned air
systems can be rapidly integrated and deployed using existing
assets to prove concepts.

How to deliver new technologies to end-users as part of an
iterative development and fielding programme.
